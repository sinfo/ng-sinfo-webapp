About

SINFO is a college student and non-profitable organization responsible for the planning of one of the biggest Tech events in Portugal. It’s an annual conference that takes place at the Alameda Campus of Técnico Lisboa, in the capital city of Portugal, Lisbon. We had a record breaking attendance of participants last year, achievement we hope to repeat next year, from February 17th to 21st 2020.

Mission

At SINFO, our mission is to give students and general attendants the opportunity to interact and get closer to influential and thriving people in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Science. We also bring tech companies of major relevance to our attendants so they can communicate with those companies’ employees, share experiences and maybe find a future position. We try to make it happen every day with dedication, by continuously improving the quality of our event, not only by bringing new speakers for every edition but also innovativing companies and startups that share our love for technology.

Vision

We live in an increasingly technological world in constant and swirling motion, where Computer Science and IT play fundamental roles. For the last 25 editions SINFO has been the bridge between that world and the student community. Every single one of them sums up several months of dedication and hard work laid out by its members, who are driven by the rewarding smiles of SINFO’s participants and the desire to surpass themselves every year. We like to think we’ve managed to do both hitherto. Our goal is to inspire and be inspired.

Target

We are mainly focused on bringing the ultimate technological experience to the student community. Nonetheless, everyone fascinated by IT or seeking a new career opportunity is welcome to join us.
